My name is Chris Fergo and I live on Long Island. I'm 21 years old and a recent Video Game Design graduate!  I‘ve been interested in 3D modeling and animation for quite some time now.

I was an avid Star Wars fan growing up and I was heavily active at www.theforce.net.  Then in 1999 they made a new section to the site that included Fan-Films.  I became intrigued.  Some of the fan-made films on the site are pretty amazing for amateur filmmakers. I was blown away by a lot of them, so I became inspired to make one myself. Long story short, I had a lot of failed attempts for a film, but I did have a lot of FX tests.  I used makeshift blue screens and green screens.  I learned how to create lightsaber glows in Photoshop (the very thing Photoshop was designed for).  But one thing I could not tackle was 3D modeling.  I tried so hard to learn on my own.  I bought books, tried to follow tutorials, fooled around with the programs but nothing was sinking in. I knew that if I was ever going to learn 3D I was going to have to go to school for it.

In April 2006, I finally found a great school where I could learn 3D modeling and animation. It was in a Game and Web design course.  I didn’t mind at all, I’ve always been a pretty big gamer myself.  Two of the most amazing games I’ve ever played are Mortal Kombat and Half-Life. While I was there I met a lot of wonderful people, two I definitely have to mention are Frankie and Mr. Mastermaker. Frankie stood by me and helps me out all the time, and I thank him for that.  He always tells me to ‘Stay on the information highway, there’s no need to take side roads.’ Mr. Mastermaker, I’m proud to say, was the teacher who not only taught me 3D modeling and animation, but made it stick. Both of these guys inspire me and are the reason why I spend so much time getting my models to look just right.

On the side I also draw and play music. I have been playing the drums for over 10 years and guitar for 5 years. I competed in NYSSMA and got involved with school and local bands, performing Classical, Rock and Heavy Metal.
